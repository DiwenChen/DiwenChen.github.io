Student Project Post App


In the Software Engineering class, I developed a student project post app in a team of four.

It was built using visual studio code, python, and flask. The app handles two user types: student and faculty.

Faculty can create project posts for students to join up. Students could browse the catalogue

and apply to interested projects. The app creates a complete experience of the process.
